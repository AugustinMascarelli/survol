Survol
Understanding legacy applications.
Copyright Primhill Computers 2017
Survol allows to display any software entity or resources running on a computer.
Its ontology and terminology are based on Common Information Model. Its interacts freely with CIM implementations such as Microsoft WMI, OpenLMI, OpenPegasus, WBEM.

Survol has two display modes:
Javascript mode, fully interactive, designed for investigating, browsing, drilling into applications internals
Print mode, where the rendered view cannot be modified but is designed to be nicely printed for reporting or presentations
